What is the Amadeus Genesis Hack?

The Amadeus Genesis Hack is an online global hackathon focused on agentic AI, commercial and privacy rails, and RISC-V compute prototyping.

The event includes:

    A design-driven ideathon track
    A deep-tech AI hardware track
    Live virtual workshops & technical deep dives
    Sponsor challenges
    6 weeks of hacking, architecture design, and prototyping


Who can participate?

Anyone with a creative or technical mindset:

    AI & ML researchers
    Blockchain engineers
    Systems and cryptography developers
    Hardware / RISC-V / FPGA engineers
    ZK & TEE practitioners
    Product designers
    Students & newcomer
